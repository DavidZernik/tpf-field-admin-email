A reminder to please log your Podcast Fellowship student hours


Thank you for using the Podcast Fellowship as a tool to connect to your students. A foundation of our success is that students are paid every week. Please follow this link to let us know which of your students completed a podcast this week so that we can send a stipend.

LOG IN HERE



Paying your students is as easy as 1-2-3:
 

Step 1


Log into the site

To log in, use your email address and the password you created. If you have any issues logging in, please email l‌eah@‌podcastfellowship.org


LOG IN


 

Step 2


Find the student(s) you wish to pay

Scroll down in the dashboard until you find the name of the student or students you met with this week, to whom you wish to request payment.


 

Step 3


Press "Pay Now"

Click "Pay Now" in the row of the student you wish to pay. This request will get sent to our administrators, who will review and accept the payment request, and send money directly to your student's Paypal account.
